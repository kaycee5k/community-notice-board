About the Project

The Community Notice Board is digital platform for neighbours to share offers and requests for support and help. Neighbours who have needs like financial, shelter, medical conditions, feeding challenges and several others are encouraged to post their needs on the platform and those who also have offers to help in any way are also encouraged to do likewise. Through this platform, we support and bear each others burden.

our goal is to make it easier for community members to connect, share resources, and support one another in times of need.
